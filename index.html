PED 2014/68/EU — Equipment Category Classifier

Determine the conformity assessment category for pressure vessels and piping based on Annex II classification tables of Directive 2014/68/EU (Pressure Equipment Directive).

Vessels: Tables 1–4 Piping: Tables 5–8 Categories I – IV Article 4(3) — SEP

Fluid Group 1

Dangerous: flammable, explosive, oxidising, acutely toxic, or TS > 350 °C for steam/gas.

Fluid Group 2

All fluids not classified Group 1 — e.g. water, air, nitrogen, non-toxic refrigerants.

PS — Max. Allowable Pressure

Gauge pressure in bar. Equipment with PS ≤ 0.5 bar is excluded from PED scope (Art. 1.6).

Key Parameters

Vessels: PS × V (bar·L). Piping: PS × DN (bar·mm).
